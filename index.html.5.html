Altayba Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of the city, M & S Restaurant, owned and managed by the Alabd Alfattah family, is more than just a dining establishment. Established in 2005, our restaurant started as a small dream and has grown into a culinary destination where flavors come alive.

Our chefs, trained in global cuisines, craft dishes that blend tradition with innovation. From our signature dishes like the "M&S Special Burger" to our seasonal specials, every plate is a testament to our dedication to quality, flavor, and presentation.

But it's not just about the food. Our team, led by the Alabd Alfattah family, is committed to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere. We believe in creating experiences, not just meals. As one of our regulars said, "Every time I walk through those doors, I feel like family."

Our Vision

To be the city's go-to spot for authentic, innovative, and memorable dining experiences, proudly led by the Alabd Alfattah family legacy.
